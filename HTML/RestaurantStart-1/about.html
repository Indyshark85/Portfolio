The History of our Restuarant

Our restuarant had its humble beginnings in 1960. When Eggy Eggman sent up his chicken farm in Egg, Austria. After running his chicken farm for many years he realized that every year there would be a surplus of eggs that he could not sell. Eggy built this restuarant with his bare hands and worked here independly at first, but the business grew beyond.
Eggy decided to start this restuarant to be able to sell his extra eggs. Today he is still running this restuarant to this day. All the eggs cooked and sold in this restuarant are from the chicken farm down the road. Our restuarant is known for our good quality service and good quality food.
